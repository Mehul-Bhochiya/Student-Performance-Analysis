## üìå Project Overview

## üìà Key Insights
- Attendance and study hours are the strongest predictors of exam performance  
- Higher motivation, parental involvement, and access to resources improve outcomes  
- Teacher quality and positive peer influence significantly support student success  
- Lifestyle factors such as sleep and physical activity show minimal direct impact  
- Students with learning disabilities require additional academic support
